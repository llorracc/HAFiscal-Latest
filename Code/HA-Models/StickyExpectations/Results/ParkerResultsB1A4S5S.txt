The bonus check is announced to sticky households one period in advance.
One period before the bonus arrives, households consume 4.7% of the bonus amount.
In the period that the bonus arrives, households consume 17.2% of the bonus amount.
One period after the bonus arrives, households consume 12.8% of the bonus amount.
Two periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 9.6% of the bonus amount.
Three periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 7.5% of the bonus amount.
Four periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 5.9% of the bonus amount.
