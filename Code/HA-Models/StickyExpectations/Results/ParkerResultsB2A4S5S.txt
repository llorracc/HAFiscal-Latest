The bonus check is announced to sticky households two periods in advance.
Two periods before the bonus arrives, households consume 5.6% of the bonus amount.
One period before the bonus arrives, households consume 8.0% of the bonus amount.
In the period that the bonus arrives, households consume 18.5% of the bonus amount.
One period after the bonus arrives, households consume 13.2% of the bonus amount.
Two periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 10.0% of the bonus amount.
Three periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 7.6% of the bonus amount.
Four periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 6.0% of the bonus amount.
