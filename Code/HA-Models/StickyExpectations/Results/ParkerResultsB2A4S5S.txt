One period before the bonus arrives, households consume 6.8% of the bonus amount.
In the period that the bonus arrives, households consume 15.7% of the bonus amount.
One period after the bonus arrives, households consume 11.3% of the bonus amount.
Two periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 8.7% of the bonus amount.
Three periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 6.7% of the bonus amount.
Four periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 5.4% of the bonus amount.
