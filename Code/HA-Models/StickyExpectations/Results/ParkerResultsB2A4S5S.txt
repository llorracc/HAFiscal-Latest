One period before the bonus arrives, households consume 7.9% of the bonus amount.
In the period that the bonus arrives, households consume 18.8% of the bonus amount.
One period after the bonus arrives, households consume 13.0% of the bonus amount.
Two periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 9.6% of the bonus amount.
Three periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 7.5% of the bonus amount.
Four periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 6.4% of the bonus amount.
